18 /**
19  * @brief Finds a vector of pairs (particle_particle_candidates) which shows the
20  * candidate particle-particle collision pairs. These collision pairs will be
21  * used in the fine search to investigate if they are in contact or not.
22  *
23  * @param[in] particle_handler The particle handler of particles in the broad
24  * search
25  * @param[in] cells_local_neighbor_list A vector (with size equal to the number
26  * of local cells) of vectors. Each sub-vector have a size equal to the number
27  * of adjacent local cells plus one. First element of each sub-vector shows the
28  * main cell itself.
29  * @param[in] cells_ghost_neighbor_list A vector (with size equal to the number
30  * of local cells) of vectors. Each sub-vector have a size equal to the number
31  * of adjacent ghost cells plus one. First element of each sub-vector shows the
32  * main cell itself.
33  * @param[out] local_contact_pair_candidates Ankerl unordered dense map. Stores
34  * potential pairs of local-local particle in contact without redundancy.
35  * Keys are particle ids and mapped types are vectors of particle ids.
36  * @param[out] ghost_contact_pair_candidates Ankerl unordered dense map. Stores
37  * potential pairs of local-ghost particle in contact. Keys are particle ids and
38  * mapped types are vectors of particle ids.
39  */
