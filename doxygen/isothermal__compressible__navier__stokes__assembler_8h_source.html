16 /**
17  * @brief Class that assembles the core of the isothermal compressible Navier-Stokes equations.
18  * According to the following weak form:
19  * \f$\nabla \cdot (\rho \mathbf{u}) + \rho \mathbf{u} \cdot \nabla \mathbf{u} -
20  * \nabla p - \mu \nabla^2 \mathbf{u} = 0\f$ with a full GLS stabilization
21  * including the laplacian of the test function.
22  *
23  * @tparam dim An integer that denotes the number of spatial dimensions
24  *
25  * @ingroup assemblers
26  */
